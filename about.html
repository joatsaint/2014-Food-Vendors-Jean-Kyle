Region 5 ESC welcomes you to the

2014 Food Show and Tasting


Region 5 ESC Food Service Coop Food Show
“SCHOOL NUTRITION ACROSS TEXAS”

Thursday, February, 20, 2014
Beaumont Civic Center
701 Main St.
Beaumont, TX 77701

PURPOSE:
To allow students, parents, administrators and food service personnel to have input into products selected for our Coop.

WHERE:
Beaumont Civic Center, 701 Main St, Beaumont, TX 77701

DATE:
Thursday, February 20, 2014

TIME:
2:00 pm until 6:00 pm (Arrival time for vendors 12:30 pm)

FEE:
$650.00 per full table;
$375.00 per half table
Payment to Region 5 ESC.
Payment due by January 24, 2014.
Fees are non-refundable in the event of tardiness or noshows.

THEME:
“School Nutrition Across Texas”
Tablecloths and some table decoration will be provided.
Please merchandise your products accordingly.

PRODUCTS:
Bring any items that you would like to exhibit, but please limit the number to Six per full table.
Evaluation forms will be provided for each vendor’s products.

PARTICIPANTS:
We expect approximately 1200 participants to attend.

PROMOTIONAL:
Feel free to give away small items such as stickers, pens, notepads, etc.
If you would like to donate door prizes (for students only), please contact Jean Kyle, Purchasing Coordinator 409-951-1782.

KITCHEN:
The Civic Center kitchen will only accommodate 6 vendors for use.
The 1st six vendors inquiring by mail, e-mail or phone will have use of the kitchen.

ATTENTION
The Civic Center will not be responsible for the disposal of grease or oil.
You will be responsible for the disposal of your product.
YOU WILL ALSO NEED TO SUPPLY ICE IF NEEDED FOR YOUR BOOTH. HAIR NETS, CAPS OR SCARVES AND GLOVES MUST BE WORN WHILE COOKING AND SERVING FOOD.

REGISTRATION:
Complete the attached form, indicate the items you have chosen to exhibit, and specify your kitchen equipment needs.
Return the form and payment by January 24, 2014.
If you are unable to participate or have any questions, please contact Jean Kyle (409) 951-1782.

VENDOR NAME TAGS:
Please submit to Jean Kyle, the names of all workers in your booth.
Due to security reasons we will supply all vendors with name tags.
All vendors will need to sign in at the door.
